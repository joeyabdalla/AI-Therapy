Steps to self diagnose if you have depression

+

These are some common signs of depression:

1. Persistent feelings of sadness or emptiness

2. Loss of interest in activities that were once enjoyable

3. Changes in appetite and weight

4. Insomnia or oversleeping

5. Fatigue or loss of energy

6. Feelings of worthlessness or excessive guilt

7. Difficulty concentrating or making decisions

8. Recurrent thoughts of death or suicide

If you experience any of these symptoms, it is important to seek help from a mental health professional.

Types of depression and recovery

+

1. Major depressive disorder: psychotherapy, antidepressant medications, lifestyle changes, electroconvulsive therapy (ECT), and transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS)

2. Persistent depressive disorder: psychotherapy, antidepressant medications, cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), and interpersonal therapy (IPT)

3. Seasonal affective disorder: light therapy, psychotherapy, and medications

4. Postpartum depression: psychotherapy, antidepressant medications, support groups, and lifestyle changes

5. Bipolar disorder: medications, psychotherapy, electroconvulsive therapy (ECT), and transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS)

Benefits of treating depression

+

1. Improves your mood

2. Increases your energy

3. Improves sleep

4. Better physical health

5. Improves relationships

6. Reduced risk of suicide
